Student Projects

Project 1: Eco - Friendly Campus Navigation App

Description: This is a navigation app specifically designed for campuses with an emphasis on environmental features. It not only provides users with the optimal walking route navigation between buildings and facilities on campus, but also recommends the use of environmentally friendly travel methods during navigation, such as encouraging the use of green trails on campus and avoiding roads with heavy traffic to reduce carbon emissions. At the same time, the application integrates the information of garbage sorting points on campus, which is convenient for users to classify garbage on the way. Through this project, students apply mobile application development skills, including the use of map apis, interface design, and the integration and presentation of environmental data, with the aim of promoting environmental awareness and mobility on campus.

Project 2: Online Learning Community Platform

Description: It is an online learning community platform designed to provide a space for learners from different disciplines to communicate and interact. The platform has rich functions, including course resource sharing, study group formation, online discussion forum and learning progress tracking. Students can post their study notes and insights on the platform, as well as initiate discussions on specific learning problems and get help from others. The project integrates various aspects of knowledge such as web development technology, database management, and social network function construction, and explores the interaction and effectiveness of online learning, facilitating learners to break geographical restrictions and share learning resources and experiences.
